TrashTag

Hybrid Mobile App for Litter Cleanups

TL;DR: Over 16 weeks, Trashtag's story unfolds through key deliverables, from vision and scope documents to weekly project logs, wireframes, and a high-definition prototype. The style guide ensures a cohesive aesthetic, leading to the deployment of the mobile app. Serving as a Project Manager and UX & UI Designer, we leverage tools like Google Docs, Google Sheets, Marvel App, Figma, and Flutter to turn Trashtag's vision into reality.

About the project

TrashTag aims to change the way people behave towards waste by creating a supportive community through education and experience. They are a new environmental initiative focused on reshaping people's approach to waste. TrashTag is an interdisciplinary group of IDC students from around the world who have joined forces with the goal of preserving the beautiful landscapes of Israel from pollution.

Entendimiento del problema

Propósito: Se están generando volúmenes crecientes de desechos a medida que aumenta la población mundial. Aún con los sistemas de eliminación, que no pueden mantenerse al día con la cantidad de desechos producidos, esto se convierte en causa de un impacto ambiental significativo.

Trashtag desea acumular información y conocimiento sobre los esfuerzos realizados por la comunidad de voluntarios y brigadistas de Israel, para así proponer soluciones viables a los problemas clave de comportamiento de la sociedad israelí (comenzando en Herzliya) hacia la basura y los desechos.
