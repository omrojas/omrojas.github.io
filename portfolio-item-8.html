TrashTag

Mobile App Design and Development

TL;DR: Over 4 months, Trashtag's story unfolds through key deliverables: from vision and scope documents to weekly project logs, wireframes, and a high-definition prototype with an style guide that led to the successful deployment of the mobile Flutter native app.

About the client

TrashTag, an Israeli environmental initiative led by an interdisciplinary group of IDC students, aims to reshape attitudes toward waste. Fostering a supportive community through education and shared experiences, TrashTag has developed an app to streamline efforts in litter collection. Our mission is to preserve Israel's scenic landscapes through responsible waste management practices.

Project definition

Growing volumes of waste are being generated as the global population increases. Even with disposal systems in place, which cannot keep up with the amount of waste produced, this becomes a significant cause of environmental impact.

Trashtag aims to gather information and knowledge about the efforts made by the volunteer community and brigades in Israel. This is to propose viable solutions to key behavioral issues in Israeli society (starting in Herzliya) concerning waste and garbage.

Deliverables

  1. Vision and Scope Document
  2. Software Requirements Specification
  3. Weekly Project Execution Logs
  4. Information Architecture
  5. Wireframes
  6. High-Definition Prototype
  7. Style Guide
  8. Mobile Application Deployment

My roles: Business Analyst and UX/UI Designer

Duration: 16 weeks (4 months)

Tools and technology: Google Workspace, Marvel App, Figma and Flutter
